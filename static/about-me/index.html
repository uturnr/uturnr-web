When I was training for Air Traffic Control, I made a couple of simple games using the Phaser HTML5 framework. One was designed to help memorize bearings (within 5 degrees!) and distances for 46 waypoints in the vicinity of the fictional "March Airport". The other was to help practice the procedure for telling one aircraft about another's relative position. I reworked the games for the Regina airport during my on-the-job training.
